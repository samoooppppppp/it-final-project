COFFEE PEOPLE LIFESTYLE

In 1999, we started off as the first specialty coffee
in Nepal. Over the years, we have diversified from a small coffee shop
to an enterprise franchising the Nepali Coffee brand, nationally and internationally.
Our commitment to quality and sustainability drives everything we do.
We work directly with coffee farmers,
ensuring fair prices and environmentally friendly practices.
At Himalayan Java Express, we believe in more than just great coffee.
We're dedicated to creating a welcoming environment where people can gather,
connect,
and enjoy the coffee culture.
